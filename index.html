Save More Lifes with you

The objects of the organization are as follows;

Education

To promote education among umuatugbuoma youths through the provision of bursary awards to deserving students; and the criteria for the disbursement of the bursary awards shall be determined by the trustees of the association.

Basic Necessities

To help the less privileged in Umuatugbuoma through the provision of food, clothing materials and shelter.

Electrical Infrastructure

To provide electricity and street lights to Umuatuoma community.

Library Facilities

To provide library facilities to Umuatugbuoma community in order to ensure that the youths have a good and secure environment for studies.

Security and Safety

To promote the security of Umuatugbuoma through the provision of CCTV, licensed gun, touch lights, motor bikes and other security gargets to enable the community neighborhood watch perform their duties of securing the lives and property within the community efficiently and effectively.

Sports Facilities

To provide sports facilities to Umuatugbuoma community.

Social Activities

The charity shall initiate and or sponsor social activities within the community in order to promote peaceful co-existence among the citizens.

Culture and Traditions of the Community

To promote the culture and tradition of the community and in furtherance of this object; (i) the organization shall ensure that all the community communal antiquities, monuments, shrines, artifacts and antiquities are preserved and updated (ii) the right to freedom of religion and worship of the individual members of the organization shall be respected.
